1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

IndoorPlant.in is a premium, AI-powered e-commerce platform exclusively dedicated
to indoor plants. The platform combines a clean, modern shopping experience with
an intelligent AI Plant Advisor that helps users discover the perfect plants for
their specific environment, lifestyle, and care capacity.

The platform is designed for the Indian market with Razorpay as the payment
gateway, INR pricing, and pan-India delivery coverage. The primary differentiator
is the conversational AI agent that gives personalized, expert-level plant
recommendations — making plant shopping accessible even for complete beginners.

Platform URL: https://www.indoorplant.in
Primary Language: English
Currency: Indian Rupee (INR ₹)
Target Launch: Q3 2026

- AI: "Hi! I'm PlantBot, your personal plant advisor. I'll help you find the

- AI: "Which room are you looking for a plant for?"

Step 3: SUNLIGHT AVAILABILITY
- AI: "How much natural light does that room get?"
- Options: No natural light (only artificial) / Low light (north-facing) /
  Medium indirect (east/west) / Bright indirect / Direct sunlight (south-facing)

Step 4: MAINTENANCE PREFERENCE
- AI: "How much time can you give to plant care?"
- Options: Minimal (I often forget!) / Low (once a week check) /
  Moderate (regular care) / High (I love detailed care routines)

Step 5: LIFESTYLE QUESTIONS
- AI: "Do you have pets at home?"
- Options: Yes, cats / Yes, dogs / Yes, both / No pets
- AI: "Do you have young children at home?"
- Options: Yes / No
- AI: "What's the primary goal for this plant?"
- Options: Air purification / Aesthetic & decor / Stress relief / Gifting /

Step 6: ADDITIONAL PREFERENCES (optional)
- AI: "Any size preference?"
- Options: Small (tabletop) / Medium (floor plant) / Large (statement piece) /
  No preference
- AI: "What's your budget?"
- Options: Under ₹299 / ₹300–₹699 / ₹700–₹1499 / ₹1500+

Step 7: RECOMMENDATIONS
- AI presents 3-5 personalized plant recommendations
- Each recommendation card shows:
  - Plant image (thumbnail)
  - Plant name
  - Match score / "Why this plant for you" explanation

Step 8: FOLLOW-UP
- AI: "Would you like me to refine these suggestions or tell you more about

- AI can answer: "How often do I water a Monstera?", "Is this plant safe for

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6.9 PAYMENT INTEGRATION — RAZORPAY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

INTEGRATION TYPE: Razorpay Standard Checkout (JavaScript SDK)

SUPPORTED PAYMENT METHODS
--------------------------
- UPI (All apps + QR code)
- Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Diners)
- Debit Cards (All major banks including RuPay)
- Net Banking (50+ banks)
- EMI (credit card + cardless EMI via Razorpay)
- Wallets (Paytm, Mobikwik, Freecharge, Ola Money)
- Buy Now Pay Later (Simpl, LazyPay, ZestMoney)
- Cash on Delivery (handled separately, not via Razorpay)

RAZORPAY FEATURES TO ENABLE
----------------------------
- Razorpay Order API (for server-side order creation)
- Razorpay Payment Links (for support team / COD replacement)
- Webhooks for payment success / failure notifications
- Razorpay Dashboard for finance team
- Automatic retry for failed payments
- Partial refund support
- Route / Smart Routing for success rate optimization

PAYMENT FLOW
------------
1. User clicks "Place Order"
2. Backend creates Razorpay Order (via server-side API)
3. Razorpay Checkout modal opens (pre-filled with name, email, phone)
4. User completes payment
5. Razorpay sends payment_id, order_id, signature to frontend
6. Backend verifies signature (HMAC SHA256)
7. On success: Order created in DB, confirmation email sent
8. On failure: Error shown, retry option provided

15. ACCESSIBILITY STANDARDS
================================================================================

Target: WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance

REQUIREMENTS
------------
- Color contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1 for body text
- All images have descriptive alt text
- All interactive elements accessible via keyboard (Tab + Enter)
- Focus indicators clearly visible
- ARIA labels on icon-only buttons (cart icon, wishlist icon, etc.)
- Skip-to-content link (for screen reader users)
- Form labels explicitly associated with inputs
- Error messages clearly state the problem and how to fix it
- No content flashes more than 3 times per second (epilepsy safety)
- Font size min 16px for body text
- Touch targets minimum 44x44px on mobile
- Semantic HTML (h1–h6 hierarchy, nav, main, section, footer, etc.)
